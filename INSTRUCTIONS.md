# Woodway Pool — How to Use the Website

**Website:** https://claudecodejg-bot.github.io/woodway-masters/

---

## Overview

The Woodway Pool website is a live leaderboard that tracks every team's estimated winnings during the Masters Tournament. Scores and standings update automatically throughout the tournament — no login or account required, just open the link and watch.

---

## The Header

At the top of every page you'll see:

- **Tournament name & round** — e.g., "Masters Tournament — Round 2 — In Progress"
- **Purse** — the total prize money up for grabs (e.g., "Purse: $20,000,000")
- **Last Updated** — timestamp of the most recent data refresh

### Search Bar
Type any part of a team name to instantly filter results down to just that team (works on the Pool Leaderboard and Teams & Picks tabs).

### Refresh Button (↻ Refresh)
The site auto-refreshes every 5 minutes, but you can tap **↻ Refresh** at any time to pull the latest scores.

---

## The Three Tabs

### 🏆 Pool Leaderboard

This is the main view — your pool standings, ranked by estimated earnings.

| Column | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **#** | Pool rank (T = tied) |
| **Team** | Team name |
| **Est. Pool Earnings** | Total estimated winnings across all active golfers |

**Top 3 rows** are highlighted in gold, silver, and bronze.

**Daily movement** — During rounds 2, 3, and 4, a small green/red badge appears next to each team's earnings showing how much they've gained or lost compared to where they stood at the end of the prior round (e.g., `+1.2mm` or `-0.8mm`).

#### Expanding a Team Row
Click any team row to expand it and see the full breakdown of that team's golfers:

| Column | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Golfer** | Player name (clickable — see Golfer Popup below) |
| **Pos** | Current tournament position (or MC / NIF) |
| **Score** | Total score to par |
| **Rounds** | Colored badges for R1–R4 scores |
| **Odds** | The multiplier assigned to this golfer (e.g., "10-1") |
| **Est. Earnings** | Prize money at current position |
| **Odds Adj Earnings** | Est. Earnings × Odds — this is the golfer's contribution to the team total |

Golfers who **missed the cut** appear at the bottom of the list and contribute $0.

Click the row again to collapse it.

### 👥 Teams & Picks

A quick-glance view of every team and their 6 golfer selections.

Each golfer appears as a colored **chip** showing the player's name and current position:

| Chip color | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| **Green** | Active — golfer is still in the tournament |
| **Gray** | Missed Cut (MC) or Projected Cut (PC) |
| **Red/Dim** | Not In Field (NIF) — golfer withdrew or wasn't in the field |

You can sort by **Team name** or **Est. Earnings** by clicking the column headers.

Click any golfer chip to open the Golfer Popup (see below).

---

### ⛳ Tournament

The full golf tournament leaderboard — every player in the field, ranked by score.

| Column | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Pos** | Tournament position |
| **Player** | Golfer name (clickable) |
| **Total** | Cumulative score to par |
| **Today** | Score so far in the current round |
| **Thru** | Holes completed today (F = finished the round) |
| **R1–R4** | Score for each individual round |
| **Est. Earnings** | Estimated prize money at current position |
| **Odds** | Pool multiplier for this player |
| **Odds Adj Earnings** | Est. Earnings × Odds (pool value) |

A **✂ cut line** divider separates active players from those who missed (or are projected to miss) the cut.

You can click any column header to sort by that column. Click again to reverse the sort.

## Golfer Popup

Click **any golfer name** on any tab to open a popup with:

- The golfer's current **position and score**
- A list of **every team that picked them**, with:
  - Their pool rank badge (e.g., **#1**, **#3**)
  - Their full 6-golfer roster shown as chips
  - The clicked golfer highlighted in **dark green**
  - Other golfers color-coded by status (active / MC / NIF)

Close the popup by clicking the **×** button or clicking anywhere outside the popup.

---

## Score Badges & Color Coding

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **MC** | Missed Cut — player is out of the tournament |
| **PC** | Projected Cut — player is on the bubble during round 2 |
| **NIF** | Not In Field — player was not in this tournament |
| Green score | Under par |
| Red score | Over par |
| White/neutral score | Even par (E) |

---

## How Pool Earnings Are Calculated

Each team's **Estimated Pool Earnings** = the sum of every active golfer's estimated prize money multiplied by their odds multiplier.

> **Example:** A golfer in 5th place earns an estimated $500,000 in prize money. If their odds multiplier is 10-1, they contribute **$5,000,000** to their team's total.

- Golfers who missed the cut contribute **$0**.
- Earnings are **estimates** based on current tournament position and standard PGA Tour payout percentages. Final payouts are determined after the tournament ends.
