How to Use the Woodway Pool Website

This site is a live leaderboard tracking every team's estimated winnings during the Masters Tournament. Scores and standings update automatically — no login required.

The Header

At the top of every page you'll see the tournament name, current round, total purse, and the last time data was refreshed.

  • Search bar — type any part of a team name to filter results instantly.
  • ↻ Refresh button — pull the latest scores on demand. The site also auto-refreshes every 5 minutes.

🏆 Pool Leaderboard

The main view — all teams ranked by estimated pool earnings. The top 3 are highlighted in gold, silver, and bronze.

Click any team row to expand it and see a full breakdown of that team's golfers:

ColumnWhat it means
GolferPlayer name — click it to open the Golfer Popup to see other teams that have that golfer
PosCurrent tournament position (or MC)

OddsThis golfer's multiplier (e.g., "10-1")

Golfers who missed the cut appear at the bottom and contribute $0. Click the row again to collapse it.

Golfer Popup — when you click a golfer name, a popup shows every team that picked that player. You can then click any other player in the popup to jump to their teams, making it easy to browse across teams player by player.

Daily movement — in rounds 2–4, a green/red badge (e.g., +1.2mm −0.8mm) shows how much each team has gained or lost vs. the end of the prior round.

👥 Teams & Picks

A quick-glance view of every team and their 6 golfer picks. Each golfer appears as a colored chip showing their name and position:

Chip colorMeaning

Click any golfer chip to open the Golfer Popup. Sort the table by clicking the Team or Est. Earnings column headers. Use the search bar at the top to find a specific team by name.

How Pool Earnings Are Calculated

Each team's Estimated Pool Earnings = the sum of every active golfer's estimated prize money × their odds multiplier.

Example: A golfer in 5th place earns ~$500,000 in prize money. If their odds multiplier is 10-1, they contribute $5,000,000 to the team total.

Golfers who missed the cut contribute $0. All earnings are estimates based on current position — final payouts are determined after the tournament ends.

Favorites

Track the teams and players you care about most. Click the ☆ star next to any team or player to favorite them.

  • Pool Leaderboard & Teams & Picks — star any team to pin it to the top under a ★ Favorites section.
  • Tournament — star any player to pin them at the top of the leaderboard.
  • Favorites are saved in your browser — they persist across visits and refreshes. Each person's favorites are independent.
  • Click the star again to unfavorite.

Tips

  • Works on mobile — all tabs and popups are touch-friendly.
  • Data refreshes automatically every 5 minutes during tournament rounds.
  • Use the search bar to quickly find your own team without scrolling.
  • Use favorites to pin your team and favorite players to the top of each tab.
  • Bookmark this page and check back any time during the tournament.
